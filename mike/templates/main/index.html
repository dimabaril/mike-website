MIKE IV

{% endcomment %}

MIKE IV is a media artist who specializes in creating visual effects using images, generative graphics, installations and audiovisual performances. His creative contributions are evident in numerous theatrical productions in which he participates as a member of the production team responsible for the video stage design. Collaborations with musicians, sound artists and performers from a variety of backgrounds allow him to convey the emotional impact of sound and movement through his video works, resulting in immersive audiovisual performances that shift the boundaries of audience perception.
